About Me

Hi! My name is Guram Gogichaishvili, and I am a post-production specialist with expertise across the entire video production pipeline.

I specialize in video editing, audio editing, color grading, motion graphics, and animation, bringing stories to life through compelling visuals and sound. I studied Filmmaking at Shota Rustaveli Theatre and Film Georgia State University and have since worked on a diverse range of media projects.

My experience includes directing, production, and post-production for various formats, including:

  • 🎬 Podcasts
  • 📺 Documentaries & TV series
  • 📊 Business marketing & educational content
  • 🎨 Animations & short-form digital content

I thrive on creating high-quality media that engages audiences, builds brands, and drives success.
